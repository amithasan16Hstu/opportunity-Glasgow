About DUCO

A research group on robust, data-driven decision-making at the University of Glasgow.

DUCO sits at the intersection of control, optimisation, and machine learning. We are interested in algorithms that can reason about uncertainty and still make transparent, reliable decisions in practice.

Research philosophy

  • Start from real decision problems and data.
  • Use theory to understand limits and guarantees.
  • Prototype tools that practitioners can actually use.

Methods we like

  • Model predictive control (MPC)
  • Stochastic & robust optimisation
  • Probabilistic modelling and Bayesian inference
  • Safe reinforcement learning

How we work

  • Collaborative supervision and mentoring.
  • Open, reproducible code and datasets where possible.
  • Regular reading groups and project meetings.
